Abstract

In this dissertation we aim at developing basic concepts regarding universally

adopted capitalization systems and interest schemes. We establish a paral-

lel between the continuous and the discontinuous accumulation systems, and

simple and compound interest schemes. We intend to demonstrate that the

calculations of income and annuities currently adopted in Brazil are made as

anatocism and that the interests of the (cid:28)nancial institutions are determined

as a result of the interest schemes that favor them unilaterally. We have de-

the simple interest scheme and avoiding anatocism, thus showing that time is

not (cid:28)ssionable for such a scheme. In the end, we suggest a way of introducing

Financial Mathematics in high school.
