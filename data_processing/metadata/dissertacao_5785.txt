This study aims to present a new formula for the calculation of volume of various

geometrical solids studied in high school, as well as others, the so-called Three-Level

the study of this subject. The proof of this study is based on the method of exhaustion,

and, therefore, the teacher who chooses to demonstrate this will introduce students to

primitive concepts of diﬀerential and integral calculus.
