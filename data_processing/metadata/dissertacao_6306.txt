Abstract

The Hyperbole and its Applications begins with a brief historical background on the

origins of the conic sections analysis, followed by a proposition of a new approach

to the conic sections studies in High School, due to the large amount of important

applications these studies have in everyday life.

The (cid:28)rst chapter of the research presents the primary concepts necessary to a good

hyperbole study understanding. After presenting this concepts the research focuses in

the evidence of the hyperbole concept, its most important elements, the rotary axis

and the hyperbole properties. Among these properties, the main object of this study is

the re(cid:29)ective hyperbole property, since it can be applied to many (cid:28)elds of study such as

Astronomy, in the Telescopes and even Navigations. Some other properties are brie(cid:29)y

mentioned during this work and others have a more detailed approach.

Finally, a largely used new coordinates system is presented - the Polar Coordinate

System. The research purpose is to infer, by the use of this new system, the equations

of the ellipse, parable and hyperbole.

Keywords: Analytical Geometry; Conics; Parable; Hyperbole; Polar coordinates.
