Abstract

This work presents a study about some Elementary Inequalities and their applications

in basic education, collected from articles, theses and books on the subject. Initially the

concept of inequalities is presented by presenting their demonstrations and later showing

their applicability through a practical example to the detriment of a more rigorous le-

arning. For the accomplishment of these demonstrations it was necessary knowledge of

elementary algebra, Euclidean geometry, geometric constructions, mathematical induc-

tion, besides several other knowledge. The appropriation and assimilation of the Theory

of Inequalities by teachers and high school students serve as justiﬁcation for this study.

Keywords: Elementary Inequalities, Demonstrations, Applications.
