Abstract: This study proposes to construct a method to calculate soccer probabilities
and compare the results with the UFMG Mathematics Department model. In the pre-
sented methodology, we assume that the number of goals scored by the teams in a match
are independent and follow a Poisson distribution, where the average reﬂects the attack

pionship, the methodology studied presented results consistent with the UFMG model.

results for the qualiﬁers for the Copa Libertadores of America, as well as for the relegated

results are below 2/3. The proposed methodology was implemented in the software R.
