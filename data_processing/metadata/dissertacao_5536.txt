This work was developed with the objective of making a temporal analysis of some

actual circumstances of the city of IporÆ, using mathematical modeling, Interpolation

and Curve Fitting by Least Squares Method. Theoretical contributions concerning the

making and political interventions. The work also shows that there is applicability of

Interpolation and Curve Fitting by Least Squares Method in High School, which can
be enriched by modeling of everyday problems.
