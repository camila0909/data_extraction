Este trabalho apresenta uma interpreta¸c˜ao geom´etrica de sistemas lineares 2 × 2,

Abstract

This work presents a geometric interpretation of 2 × 2, 2 × 3 and 3 × 3 linear
systems, analyzing the relative positions of lines and planes that each system equation
represents and, for such graphical representation, we use the GeoGebra software. We
also approach the concept of diagonalization of linear operators and matrices, with
application in the recognition of conics (ellipse, hyperbole and parabola).

Keywords: Linear Systems, Diagonalization, Conics.
