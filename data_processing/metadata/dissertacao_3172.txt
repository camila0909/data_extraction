Abstract

In this work we present a way of treating the converse of the Pythagoras Theorem.

We will make one of the many proofs of the theorem, a proof of its converse in that we

use the theorem and, ﬁnally, a proof of converse without using the theorem, only using

algebraic and geometric properties.

to have a better understanding of the proposed problem. With this, we intend to oﬀer

a more in-depth view of a material that can be used by elementary school teachers and

that can positively inﬂuence the learning of our students.

Keywords: Theorem of Pythagoras, Converse of the Pythagoreas Theorem, application

examples.
