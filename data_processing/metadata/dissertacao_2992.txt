The matrices are object of study in Basic Education and Higher Education, but, although
they are related to several applications in Mathematics, Engineering, Computer Graphics,
and Economics, for example, in High School are approached superficially, mechanically

relevant point is the differentiated proposal for solving learning situations contained in the
curriculum support material of the State of S√£o Paulo. Also will be elaborated activities

high school students an alternative that Extends the matrix perspective efficiently and
significantly.
