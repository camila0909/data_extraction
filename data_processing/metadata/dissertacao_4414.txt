Abstract

This article proposes a discussion about the Heron triangles. A triangle is con-

sidered Heronian if it has the measures of its sides length and its area positive integers

numbers. It will be showed that every rectangle triangle of integer number side length is

heronian and these one are easy to determine, what does not happen to the other mem-

bers of this family. It will be also presented diverse methods to generate these triangles,

and exploit its properties. it will be concluded with the presentation of two activities that

can be applied in basic education.
