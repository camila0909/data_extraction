Abstract

In this work we explore the Tower of Hanoi Classic problem, the Towers of Double Hanoi,

the Tower of Cyclic Hanoi and the Tower of Hanoi with 4 pins. For this, we use the

concepts of ﬁrst and second order linear recurrences in order to determine a mathematical

of a didactic material that can be used by high school teachers, who want to insert in their

classes the classic problem of the Tower of Hanoi, as well as the curious variations of this

problem.
