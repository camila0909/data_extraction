In this work we make a study of the history of Euclidean Geometry, stating its postulates
and emphasizing the uniqueness of the ﬁfth postulate of Euclid. We will explore the most im-
portant attempts to prove the ﬁfth postulate and how this led to the creation of other Geometry,

the presentation of various activities that can be applied to students of Basic Education, consi-
dering from the ﬁxation of concepts to applications of Spherical Geometry in Geography and
daily life. The theoretical basis for the preparation of the activities is the van Hiele Model to
the development of geometric thought.
