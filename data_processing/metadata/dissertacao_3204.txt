Theorems: The Pythagorean Theorem. Initially, we will look at a historical background

natural numbers that characterize the three sides of right triangle. We will notice that
Euclidean geometry does not explain everything that occurs in our surroundings yet, the
proof is the study of spherical geometry, where we also (cid:12)nd the right triangle. Finally,
we will see how little the Pythagorean Theorem is required in the Enem tests through an
analysis of all tests already applied.
