ABSTRACT

In this work we present the Cycloid Curves: Cycloid, Epicycloid and Hypocycloid, making

their parametrizations and building these curves with the support of GeoGebra software.

In addition, we highlight properties and applications of these ﬂat curves. Speciﬁcally,

High School. Some applications are related to the knowledge of the contents: uniform

rectilinear motion, uniformly varied rectilinear motion, circular motion, force, energy,

parametric equations of a line, trigonometric ratios, length and area of circle. A classroom

experimentation was performed to present some properties of the Cycle Curves, since

the demonstration of these properties covers concepts whose formal notation is speciﬁc

subject of higher education. This experiment served to show that Cycloid Curves can be

approached in a introductory way with the content of High School and that the motivation

of the teacher is fundamental in the learning process.
