Abstract

This work has as theme to investigate the use of Geogebra software as a tool for teaching
and learning in analytic geometry, their interaction and research opportunities of this
content. It is reported the presence of computers in school and the interest that students
have for this theme, as well as the relationship between school, student and teacher
with the technologies. The Software Geogebra and how to use its tools and examples
of how to use them in solving activities. The text has contents of analytic geometry
and a host of activities resolved using the software, along with the goals, the di(cid:30)culties
encountered and the resolution of each strategy. The proposed activities have been applied
to students who attend the 3rd year of high school, that is, where the student starts
studying analytic geometry, second the school curricula. In the text appears reports on
how was the application of classroom activities.

Keywords: Teaching of analytic geometry, Education Technology, Software Geogebra.
