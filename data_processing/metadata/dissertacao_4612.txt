Abstract

Mathematics is seen by many students as an insurmountable obstacle. However, fre-

quently, the problem is not the content taught in school, but in the way these contents

are taught by many of the teachers. Therefore, the need arises to rescue their origin,

or rather, the demonstration of rules and formulas. Inequalities in particular those in-

volving right convex functions and left concave functions deserve attention because they

involve sequences of mathematical procedures to be demonstrated, even if each step to

be shown, in principle, is not very complex. Thus, these inequalities serve to exemplify

the apparently simple theorems of statements that require certain precautions, such as

mathematical reasoning sequences, and prior knowledge. The main objective of this work

is to strictly, the important numerical inequalities through the right convex functions and

left concave functions.

Keywords: Numerical inequalities, convex functions, concave functions
