ABSTRACT

MAZIOZEKI DE OLIVEIRA, Carlos Alberto. STEWART´S THEOREM AND HERON´S
THEOREM AND THE CALCULUS OF THE AREA OF A TRIANGLE IN FUNCTION OF
SIDES. 65 f. Dissertac¸ ˜ao – Programa de Mestrado Proﬁssional em Matem´atica em Rede Naci-

This dissertation was organized from the observation that mathematical theorems as Stewart’s
theorem and Heron’s theorem are rarely used in mathematics classes in middle and high schools.
The contributions of these mathematicians in calculating cevians and area of triangles can sim-
plify the solution of many problems. As a starting point, we prepared one extracurricular ac-
tivity containing four questions centered on Stewart’s theorem and applied the same in three
high school classes of CPM-PR. From the analysis of the results of this activity, we deﬁned the
literature, the text structure and organization of a collection of applied problems.

Keywords: Stewart´s theorem, Heron´s theorem, triangles, cevians, area, the four tangent cir-
cumferences problem.
