Abstract

problem, which consists in determining the inﬁnite summation of the inverse squares of

natural numbers (N = {1, 2, 3, ...}). In this work we show a method to present a didactic

a computational veriﬁcation to observe its convergence, followed by a presentation of an

actual proof to its result. The results of the problem studied in this work were extracted

of the articles [3] and [7].
