Abstract

In this work we studied the method to solving linear equations system, presented
in the book titled ”The nine chapters on the mathematical art”, which was written in
the ﬁrst century of this era. This work has the intent of showing how the mathematics
history can be used to motivate the introduction of some topics in high school. Through

system, solution of linear equations, determinants and matrices, besides the Laplacian
development for determinants calculations of square matrices of order bigger than 3,
