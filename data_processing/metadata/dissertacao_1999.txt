The teaching of mathematics, according to the Brazilian reality, requires special

Geogebra software; analysis of the results after the application of the workshop; a

simple interview with math teachers, about teaching of rational numbers; exposition

of the way in which the set of rational numbers is presented in learning books; and

ﬁnal considerations.
