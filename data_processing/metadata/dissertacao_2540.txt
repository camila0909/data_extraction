In this work, sequence concepts are presented, which are necessary for the study of ﬁrst and
second order linear equations of recurrence and their convergence. Also, the nonlinear recur-
rence equations are analyzed through the Lamerey graphs. Finally, a classroom application is
presented, which aims to show that some of these tools, that are studied in High School, can
be useful in day-to-day issues. The speciﬁc purpose of the application is to explore the theory
of recurrence equations to model the family budget and motivate students to seek strategies,
such as control of the use of electric energy, to reach their ﬁnancial objective. The GeoGebra
software were be used for students to predict the budget of their families in different situations.

Keywords: high school, application, GeoGebra, stability
