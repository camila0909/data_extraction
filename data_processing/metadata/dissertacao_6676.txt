Abstract

This work has as main focus, the LU factorization. But in order to have a good understanding of this

topic, we will also address the concept of matrices, elementary operations and scheduling. With these

prerequisites, we will see that the LU factorization is an application of one of the elementary operations

to the identity matrix In. We will see some applications of the concepts above, such as, LU factorization
without transposition of lines, the resolution of linear systems by LU factorization, permutation matrix

and the procedure of calculating the inverse of the matrix.

Keywords: Elementary Operations. Scheduling. LU Factorization.
