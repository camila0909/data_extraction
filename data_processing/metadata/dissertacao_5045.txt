Abstract

This study sought to develop a teaching sequence capable of narrowing the con-

tents of Limits, Continuity and the Intermediate Value Theorem for use in secundary

education classes. For this purpose, didactic separate proposals were built for these

themes, which were applied to students of Secondary Education of the Nicanor Souto

Maior School. The application analysis of the proposal pointed out that the use of the

sequence is viable from a didactic point of view, the possible inclusion of these con-
tents in secundary education. As for the diﬃculties to develop the proposed activities
without the help of GeoGebra, these were identiﬁed mainly on the construction of the

graph, as has been reported in the literature. The GeoGebra proved to be a signiﬁcant

tool to assist in the construction of such contents. Were proposed for future studies,

research projects that identify mathematical activities that may be strengthened with

the use of GeoGebra and researches that could results in new teaching situations that

may relate in an interdisciplinary way many areas of knowledge.

Keywords: GeoGebra. Limit. Continuity. The Intermediate Value Theorem.
