Abstract

This paper presents some methods to determine the solution of a linear recurrence. These

are divided into two categories, being called the traditional method those that use the compo-

sition of exponential functions and those that the solution is obtained by the use of generating

functions. The other is the so-called BenTaher-Rachidi method which consists of an approach

to invert Vandermonde matrices and consequently to obtain the particular solution of linear

recurrences. Finally, with Scilab software, some of the advantages and disadvantages of the

approach through the method presented by BenTaher-Rachidi are highlighted.

Keywords: Linear recurrence, Generating Function, Vandermonde matrix.
