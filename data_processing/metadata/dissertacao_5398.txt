Abstract

This is a study on Elliptic Curves and its application in speciﬁc educacional context, namely,

the High School. This work aims to present an introductory text on Elliptic Curves, as well as

some of the theoretical currents that base them. Elliptic Curves are plane curves of degree 3 can

be equipped with an operation of Abelian group deﬁned geometrically. With a simple approach,

we start the study with deﬁnitions, properties, examples and graphs of algebraic plane curves.

Then, we analyze the intersection of two curves so that next we can make an algebraic and

geometric treatment of rational points of an Elliptic Curve. Thus, we show its rich arithmetic

and algebraic structure. At the end of the dissertation, we present some activities involving

Elliptic Curves as a proposal to be developed with High School students.

Keywords: Algebraic Curves, teaching Mathematics, Elliptic Curves.
