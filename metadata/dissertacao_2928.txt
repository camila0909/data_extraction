Abstract

The present work shows a brief history regarding the number (cid:25). Let’s look at some
ideas developed from the quest to understand and calculate this important number

that has fascinated mathematicians since antiquity. We begin by approaching the irra-
tionality of (cid:25), and then recall the classic Greek problem of Circle Quadrature and how

developed by Archimedes, Nicholas of Cusa, Leibniz, Machin and Wallis, through
which we can calculate the number (cid:25) very quickly and accurately. Finally, we will do a
comparative analysis of the methods seen, displaying some charts and approximation
