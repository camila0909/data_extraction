ABSTRACT

The main goal of this coursework is to be a brief introduction to Algebra. In the very ﬁrst part
the concept of permutation was introduced and deﬁned as a bijective function from a group
in itself. After that, the concept of group and subgroup were exhibited and discussed, as well
as their basic properties. In the third part of this work the concepts of homomorphism and
isomorphism were exposed and discussed in order to pave the way for the demonstration of
Cayley’s Theorem, which was studied in the last chapter. To ﬁnish the work it was presented an
application of the content covered here in a high school class.
