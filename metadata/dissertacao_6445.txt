Abstract

The present study aimed to develop and apply a didactic sequence about teaching and

learning recursive sequences in elementary school. In this sense, we developed a sequence

of activities that was applied to 23 9th grade students of a public school in the city of

Ananindeua, Par(cid:19)a. We applied a diagnostic test to the class and the results showed that

the students have little or no knowledge about the algebraic representation of a recursive

sequence, despite being able to identify the numerical pattern in sequences. Based on

these results, we built and applied a sequence of activities, using as research methodology

the principles of Didactic Engineering. After the application of the didactic sequence,

we applied a post-test to check if the students’ learning had been satisfactory and the

analysis of the results were signi(cid:12)cant and showed that the students had good results,

especially in the algebraic identi(cid:12)cation of recursive sequences, thus, we conclude that the

didactic sequence applied contributed positively to the learning of recursive sequences.
