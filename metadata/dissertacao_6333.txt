Abstract

In this work we present a study on linear systems. We begin with the
study of systems 2x2, 2x3 system and 3x3 systems, and using geometric ar-
guments we prove that for these systems there occurs a, and only one of
the alternatives: the system has a single solution, the system has inﬁnites
solutions or the system does not have solution. We also present results that
establish algebraic conditions on the coeﬃcients of the systems for each one
of instances cited. In the sequence we present some methods of resolution
and discuss some applications.

key words: Linear systems in high schools; Methods of resolutions; Ap-

plications of linear systems.
