This work aims to make a study of Analytical  Geometry with  a vector approach in 

High School. We tried to describe a little of the history of Geometry and Analytical Geometry,

showed commitment and dedication, in addition to a lot of effort in learning some topics of 

Analytical Geometry with this approach. The results show that it is possible to apply the vector 

approach in  Analytical  Geometry in  High School,  giving students  alternatives for problem
