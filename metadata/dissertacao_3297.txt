Abstract

In this work we study the concept, the main properties and some applications of convex

functions of a real variable, where our main objective was to show its use in problems

involving inequalities. We demonstrate the Jensen, Bernoulli, Young, H¨older, Minkowsk

inequalities and studied the power means, culminating in the inequalities between means.

Finally, we present a section with problems involving maxima or minima, in which we

apply the techniques studied throughout this work.

Key-words: Convex functions, Inequalities, Means, Basic Education.
