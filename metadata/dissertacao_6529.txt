Abstract

One of the great challenges in the teaching process of Mathematics in Basic Edu-
cation has been to minimize the high de(cid:28)cit in learning this subject, especially in public
schools. Some factors such as teacher education, pedagogical practice, public policies,

seek to improve learning rates, through the application of attractive methodologies, which
