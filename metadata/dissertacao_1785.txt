Abstract

This work is a study on hyperbolic functions, and it has been developed through analogy

with circular trigonometric functions. This dissertation tells a brief story on hyperbolic

functions since its establishment. This work aims to introduce the proposed content in the

Mathematics curriculum programme, more precisely for the 3rd grade of High School, as

well as hyperbolic functions applications, for instance on pictures of planet Earth’s catenary

trajectory. Such a study would be possible based on the fact that students already have

knowledge of trigonometric functions, and also the knowledge of homothety, which is taught

in Elementary School. The knowledge in conic sections, speciﬁcally the hyperbole, and the

proposed insertion would facilitate the continuity of such functions study in higher education

courses.

key words: Hyperbolic Functions, Mathematics Education, Catenary, High school, Ap-

plications.
