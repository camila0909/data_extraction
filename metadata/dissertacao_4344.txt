ABSTRACT

The study in this dissertation, seeks to present the conic sections, emphasizing an
approach by means of a synthetic and elementary geometry, where the work is carried
out as follows: begins with a historical approach, as well as their relationship with the
circular cone; then it’s done a synthetic study on the conical exclusively on the plan; It
presents some quadric surfaces; the general equation of the second degree is presented
as an algebraic representation of a conic and are shown several situations where the
conical arise so, curiously, natural, in addition to numerous practical applications in
various ﬁelds of knowledge.

Keywords: Conic sections. Circular cone. Plan. Quadric surfaces. General
equation of the second degree. Applications.
