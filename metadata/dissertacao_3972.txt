Abstract

In this work, we present the Chinese Remainder Theorem, beginning in the requisites: con-

gruence, congruence systems, small Theorem of Fermat ending up with Chinese Remainder

Theorem, with its historical part, importance and applicability, especially, its usefulness in num-

ber theory. The main application of Chinese Remainder Theorem shown is the possibility of

working with high cardinality numbers.

Keywords: Congruence, Congruence Systems, Chinese Remainder Theorem.
