ABSTRACT

High failure rates in Diﬀerential and Integral Calculus 1 are a tradition
according to historical studies of this discipline and many attempts to reduce
failure have been proposed in the pedagogical environment and in research.
As failure is a consequence of mistakes made by students in tests, the

teaching and learning process. Thus, the present master’s dissertation aims
