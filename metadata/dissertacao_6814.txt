situations. The activities script was elaborated constituing a series of exercises that

provided a constructive, diferente and effective study on the content. In addition to 

inserting problem situations to bring mathematics closer to the studentsâ€™ daily lives, 

we used the GeoGebra software for visualization. At the end of the proposal, a new 

evaluative activity was applied, which encompassed the same contents covered in 

the activities script, for the effective proof that this suggested proposal contributes
