ABSTRACT

The objective of this work is to characterize the polynomials in mathbb Q [x] that have
roots in the unary circle. From this characterization we will estimate how many are these
roots. For this, we will establish a correspondence between the family of palindromic
polynomials P (x) of degree 2m and their respective Chebyshev transformations. This
will allow to relate the number of roots of P (x) in the unit circle with the quantity of
real roots of the Chebyshev transform of P (x) in the interval [-2,2]. Finally, with the aid
of the Descartes Rule of Signals, we will estimate the amount of roots of the Chebyshev
transform in the said range. This work was guided by the title article: ”Roots in unity
circle” by author Keith Conrad.
