Abstract

The objective of this paper is to present a proposed approach of Diﬀerential and Integral
Calculus in Basic Education to assist in learning of Physics. As a tool we use the software of
Dynamic Geometry, The Geogebra, enabling through construction and graphical analysis,
working intuitive concepts of speed, average and instantaneous acceleration, to formalize
concepts of average rate of change and instanteneous variation of functions, among others.
The suggested approach aids in the understanding of physical phenomena, because it is
through the Diﬀerential and Integral Calculus that we deﬁne the quantities involved in the
Kinematics.
