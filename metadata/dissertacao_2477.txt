The study aims to  perform a systematic analysis of the errors  committed by  students  of the

errors  categorized  by  classes.  The  conclusion  of  this  work  reveals  that  the  teacher's  view 

regarding  the  error  should  be  in  the  intention  of  using  it  as  a  feedback  of  the  student  in
