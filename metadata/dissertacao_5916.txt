Abstract

Taking in account that a mathematical concept has several forms of representation and

in order to contribute to the teaching of linear systems of equations and linear inequalities

in basic education, our purpose in this dissertation is to discuss the geometric meaning of

linear systems. In such an approach we make a presentation on the analytical geometry in the

plane and three-dimensional space, vectors in the plane and in space, matrices, determinants,

Cramer’s rule and the scaling method. We limit ourselves to dimensions two and three so that

the geometric intuition is accessible to students of basic education.

Keywords: Linear Equations Systems, Linear Inequalities, Analytic Geometry, Vectors,

Linear Combination; Scaling Method ;Cramer’s Rule.
