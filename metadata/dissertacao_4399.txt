ABSTRACT

vii

This work presents the concept and applications of one real variable convex

functions. The main goal is to show their use in problems involving inequalities.

In this way, it was established the relationship between the arithmetic and

geometric means and proved Jensen’s inequality, Young’s inequality, H¨older’s

inequality and Minkowski’s inequality. These inequalities provide a powerful

mathematical tool both in the statement as in the resolution of problem

situations that occur frequently in national and international Math Olympics.

We also use them in some cases of optimization. In addition, this work aims to

be a theoretical framework to be used both in regular classes as in training

classes for math olympics to help students who are looking for strategies

to increase their performance and provide a way for teachers to expand the

teaching practice in the classroom.

Keywords: Convex Function.

Inequality. Math Olympics. Optimization.

Teaching Practice.
