Abstract

In this work we discuss the teaching of complex numbers in high school, seeking to as-

sociate the historical context from the emergence of this type of number, in addition to

retrieving important contributions of notable mathematicians related to this subject. We

understand that the study of complex numbers can not be overlooked, given its impor-

tance and possibility of being directly related to several mathematical concepts such as

analytical geometry, algebra, vectors and linear algebra, among others. This is, from

our point of view, the purpose of this work, which brings basic concepts about complex

numbers and some possible applications.

Keywords: History of Mathematics. Complex number. Teaching. Applications.
