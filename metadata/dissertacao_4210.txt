Abstract

In mathematics, when you can associate the concrete with the abstract, reap many

fruits. Especially if in the classes are present students with visual impairment. Given

the di(cid:30)culties encountered to teach Plane Geometry on Elementary School, this work

a brief study on visual impairment, advances in legislation for inclusion of people with

disabilities in the education system and the Braille code. Were also suggested some
activities, adapted to the Multiplane.
