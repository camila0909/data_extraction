Abstract

This project is about an educational activities proposal concerning concepts re-

lated to: the opposite vertex angles; angles formed between two parallel lines cut by a

transversal line; sum of the internal and external angles of a triangle; congruency and

sameness of the triangle, among others, using the software Geogebra. The main aim is to

constructing the geometrical concepts highlighted on the topic, which gradually have pro-

gress by means of complexity and might be applied in basic education and qualiﬁcation

courses for mathematics teachers. These activities will enable the learners to have better

observation and acquire representation of concepts by building, visualizing and comparing

the elements of plane geometric ﬁgures.
