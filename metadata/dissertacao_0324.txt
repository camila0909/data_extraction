ABSTRACT

In the present work we approach one of the most important theorems of linear algebra,
called spectral theorem. The goal in prove it for symmetric operators in Rn. In didactic
linearly depedent vec-
terms, we started the work by bringing basic notions, such as:
tors, inner product in the vector space in which we emphasize the properties of the inner

and discuss limits of sequences and subsequences. Then, we comment on the monotone
sequences that can be of the type: increasing, decreasing, nonicreasing and nonidecrea-

most important theorems for the proof of the spectral theorem, which is the Bolzano-
Weierstrass theorem in the real case. Throughout the work we present examples for a
better understanding of the concepts covered.
