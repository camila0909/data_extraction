Abstract

The advent in 1801 of the brilliant work Disquisitiones Arithmeticae of Carl Gauss

Friedrich (1777-1885) provided extremely important elements to Number Theory, inclu-

the study of congruence in a systematic way, in order to the context of basic education,

by proposing didactic sequence, and their application to everyday problems.
