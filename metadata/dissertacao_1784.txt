This  dissertation  presents  an  exploratory  research  about  conceptions  of

fundamental  ideas  about  real  numbers.  The  data  reveal  that  the  need  to  (re)visit, 
(re)think  and  (re)construct  concepts  related  to  real  numbers  is  urgent,  since  many

objectives  and  approaches  of  the  subjects  of  Real  Analysis  in  the  Pre  service 
teachers  training  in  Mathematics,  understanding  its  role  in  the  mathematical 
formation for the teaching in Basic Education.
