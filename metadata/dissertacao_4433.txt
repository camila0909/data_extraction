Abstract

This paper aims to present an methodology of teaching of algebra associated to the
geometry, based on the theory of symmetry groups D4 with emphasis on the Klein
group, as perceive that the game structure "peg solitare"has the same principles
governing the Klein theory associated with symmetry group D4; so we explored the
teaching resources oﬀered by this popular game to introduce basics of groups and the
relationship between their algebraic and geometric aspects, making them accessible
to high school students.

Keywords:
