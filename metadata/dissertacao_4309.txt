ABSTRACT

This work approaches the use of Learning Objects (LOs) facing the teaching of Combi-
natorics and how these objects can help in the learning process of students. Accordingly,
the aim of this work is to check that the use of LOs in Math classes promotes a better
assimilation of the contents related to Combinatorics, seeking to awaken in the student

volving 20 students of the senior year of High School from School Jo˜ao Mattos, located

arrangement and combination. These data were organized and analyzed with the help
of Microsoft Oﬃce Excel 2010 program. The results show that the group that had a
diﬀerentiated approach (experimental) obtained a better performance compared to the
group that had a traditional approach (control).
