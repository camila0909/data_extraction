The discovery of quartenions by the mathematician Willian Rowan Hamilton (1805-1865) al-
lowed a new approach regards solving algebraic equations, providing a broad algebraic structure

the number of roots can be greater than the degree and often can be inﬁnite. We give empha-

obtaining a quaternary root of a quaternion and a real number.
