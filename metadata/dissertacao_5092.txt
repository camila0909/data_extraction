Abstract

In this study we present a project, designed as a methodology for teaching geometric
constructions, developed with a group of students of the 3rd year of high school in a
public school. Initially we reﬂect on the history of the teaching of Geometry in Brazil
from the early twentieth century, analyzing the key facts that contributed positively or
negatively to the approach of geometric constructions in schools. This research, which
is exploratory and qualitative and quantitative character was developed by conducting
classes, which describe, as well as deﬁnitions and necessary for the implementation of
the same buildings. In order to evaluate the eﬀectiveness of the project, we applied two
tests, anterior and posterior classes. Finally, enjoying the results and from observations
made during classes, we weave considerations, highlighting the possibilities of working
with geometric constructions as an ally in learning the concepts and understanding of the
properties, as well as a motivator in the teaching-learning Geometry.

Key words: Geometric Constructions. Methodology. Teaching-learning.
