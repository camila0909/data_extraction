This work presents the Brahmagupta’s formula for cyclic quadrilateral (ins-

an extension to any convex quadrilateral. Heron’s formula for area of trian-

focus of this thesis is to apply such content in class in high school.

Keywords: Brahmagupta’s formula, cyclic quadrilateral, quadrilateral ins-
