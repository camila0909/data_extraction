Abstract

This paper addresses the basic and intermediate knowledge of ordinary diﬀerential equations
(ODE) to the second order. To ﬁrst order ODE solutions, methods of separable variables
were studied, integrating factors, exact equations, Bernoulli equation and Riccati equations.
In the case of second-order ODE they were ﬁrst studied the homogeneous case with constant
coeﬃcients, after we studied the inhomogeneous case using the method of undetermined
coeﬃcients and for cases with variable coeﬃcients, we studied the variation of parameters
method. For more general cases, we use the method of power series.

Keywords: Ordinary Diﬀerential Equations; Power Series.
