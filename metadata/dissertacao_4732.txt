Abstract

The present work aims at encouraging the study of mathematics through encryption. Regarding the

frequent lack of interest of students in the subject, the study of encryption can aid cognitive development

of students in high school, making Maths more attractive. To achieve this, it was necessary to introduce

basic concepts of the Number’s Theory so as to help in the study of cryptographic techniques, be them rudi-

mentary ones, such as Displacement Cypher, Substitution Cypher, Delastelle’s Bigapped Cypher, Columnar

Transposition and Matias Sandorf’s Mask, or be them more advanced methods, such as Related Cypher or

Hill’s Cypher. In the ﬁnal part of the article, we described a short course in which the main approach is

the introduction of Number’s Theory in high school in a playful way, thus, concepts involving encryption

techniques were paramount.

Keywords: congruence, Residual Classes, Arithmetic, Encryption.
