Abstract

In this work we present a study on oblique cylinders and cylinder trunks . We analyse

the curves that delimit the sections of a cylinder determined by planes not parallel to its

axis, which are ellipses, and we present a way to calculate the perimeter and the area of

ellipses. We study parameterizations of curves in the plane and in the space, and of surfaces

in space. We construct cylinders and their plani(cid:12)cations with the aid of GeoGebra. We

propose sequences of lesson plans, for middle or high school, involving the study of oblique

cylinders and cylinder trunks and the use of GeoGebra software in the classroom. We (cid:12)nish

this work by reporting how the application of the suggested sequence of lesson plans occurred,

the procedures adopted and the results achieved.

Keywords: Cylinder. Area Calculation. Perimeter Calculation. Parameterization.

Truncated Cylinder Construction. Oblique Cylinder Construction.
